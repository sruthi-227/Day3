Geetha Madhuri

Geetha Madhuri is a celebrated figure in the Telugu music industry, renowned for her soulful voice and versatile singing style. Born on August 24, 1989, in Palakollu, Andhra Pradesh, her journey into music began with a passion nurtured from childhood. She rose to prominence through the reality show "Sydney Nagaram" and made her playback debut with the film "Vedam" in 2010, marking the beginning of a prolific career. Geetha Madhuri's ability to effortlessly transition between classical, folk, and contemporary genres has earned her acclaim and a dedicated fan base. Her melodious renditions in songs like "Melikalu" and "Gunde Jaari Gallanthayyinde" have become chartbusters, solidifying her place as one of Telugu cinema's most sought-after singers.

Beyond her musical achievements, she has ventured into judging television shows and continues to captivate audiences with her emotive vocals and enduring charm. Geetha Madhuri remains a beloved icon whose contributions enrich the vibrant landscape of "South Indian music".
